    Rules

  • This page in particular is only playable by 2 players
  • Each players take turn rolling a number from 1 - 6 (Starting from player 1)
  • Whenever a player steps on the base of a ladder, they get to climb up the top of the ladder
  • Whenever a player steps on the head of a snake, they fall down at the tail of the snake
  • Rolling above 100 will roll you back based on the amount of excess steps
